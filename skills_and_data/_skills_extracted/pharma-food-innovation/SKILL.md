# Pharma-Food Innovation & Regulatory Compliance Skill

This skill provides a structured approach to innovation, research and development, and regulatory compliance for products in the Pharmaceutical, Food, Multi-ingredient Extraction, and Cosmetic sectors. It integrates best practices for ideation, technical development, quality assurance (cGMP), and market registration, alongside critical considerations for facility operations.

## Core Workflows

This skill guides you through the following key phases:

1.  **Ideation & Brainstorming**: Generate and refine new product concepts.
    *   See [references/brainstorming.md](references/brainstorming.md) for detailed frameworks and techniques.

2.  **Research & Development (R&D) and Implementation**: Conduct technical development, formulation, and process optimization.
    *   See [references/rd-implementation.md](references/rd-implementation.md) for R&D workflows and cGMP integration.
    *   See [references/technical-protocols.md](references/technical-protocols.md) for detailed extraction technologies, manufacturing protocols (lyophilization, spray drying), and raw material processing.

3.  **Facility & Production Suitability**: Address critical aspects of manufacturing environment, utility management, and waste handling.
    *   See [references/utility-requirements.md](references/utility-requirements.md) for guidelines on utilities (e.g., HVAC, WFI, clean steam).
    *   See [references/waste-management.md](references/waste-management.md) for strategies on waste stream characterization and disposal.
    *   See [references/production-suitability.md](references/production-suitability.md) for process validation, equipment qualification, and facility design principles.

4.  **Regulatory Compliance & Market Registration**: Navigate complex regulatory landscapes for product approval and market entry.
    *   See [references/regulatory-cgmp.md](references/regulatory-cgmp.md) for general cGMP principles.
    *   See [references/regulatory-us-fda.md](references/regulatory-us-fda.md) for specific US FDA requirements.
    *   See [references/regulatory-sfda.md](references/regulatory-sfda.md) for specific SFDA requirements.

## How to Use This Skill

Begin by identifying your current phase in the product lifecycle (ideation, R&D, facility setup, or regulatory). Then, consult the relevant reference document for detailed guidance, checklists, and best practices. This skill will help you generate ideas, structure your development process, ensure your products meet the stringent quality and safety standards required for global markets, and establish a compliant and efficient production environment.
