# Facility Zoning & Flow Standards
Guidelines for designing pharmaceutical-grade biotech facilities with integrated warehouse and lab systems.

## 1. The 3-Zone Cascading Pressure System
| Zone | Function | Pressure Type | ISO Class |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Zone 1** | Raw Material Pre-Processing | Negative (-) | Industrial |
| **Zone 2** | High-Tech Extraction & R&D | Neutral (0) | ISO Class 8 |
| **Zone 3** | Sterile Manufacturing & Filling | Positive (+) | ISO Class 7 |

## 2. Warehouse & Dispensing Integration
- **Quarantine Area**: Mandatory for all incoming raw materials awaiting QC approval.
- **QC Sampling Room**: Isolated room within the warehouse for safe sampling.
- **Dispensing Suite**: A cleanroom environment for precise weighing and staging of materials for production.

## 3. Personnel & Material Flow
- **Airlocks**: Must be used between all zones to maintain pressure differentials.
- **Unidirectional Flow**: Personnel should enter through gowning and exit through separate de-gowning to prevent cross-contamination.
- **Material Pass-Throughs**: Use interlocking hatches for moving materials between zones.
