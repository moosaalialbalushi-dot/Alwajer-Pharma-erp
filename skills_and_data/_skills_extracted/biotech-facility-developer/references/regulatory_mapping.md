# Global Biotech Regulatory Mapping
Use this reference to map compliance requirements for international biotech and cosmetic exports.

## 1. US FDA (MoCRA 2022)
- **Facility Registration**: Mandatory biennial registration for facilities manufacturing cosmetics for US distribution.
- **Product Listing**: Mandatory listing of each cosmetic product, including ingredients.
- **Responsible Person**: Must be designated for adverse event reporting and safety substantiation.
- **GMP Compliance**: Must adhere to upcoming FDA-mandated Good Manufacturing Practices.

## 2. GCC (SFDA & MoH)
- **eCosma System**: Mandatory registration for products entering Saudi Arabia.
- **GSO 14644**: Standards for cleanrooms and associated controlled environments.
- **Halal Certification**: Critical for market acceptance in the Middle East and SE Asia.

## 3. SE Asia (BPOM, FDA Philippines, etc.)
- **ASEAN Cosmetic Directive (ACD)**: Harmonized requirements for ingredient safety and labeling.
- **Product Information File (PIF)**: Must be maintained and available for audit.
