# Biotech Facility Developer

This skill formalizes the end-to-end process for developing high-tech biotech facilities, from raw material sourcing to global export readiness.

## 1. Strategic Discovery & Regulatory Mapping
- **Identify Target Markets**: Map the regulatory requirements for all target regions (e.g., US FDA MoCRA, GCC SFDA, ASEAN ACD).
- **Technical Parameters**: Define exact extraction and processing settings (e.g., pH, temperature, pressure for CO2 extraction).
- **Resource**: Refer to `references/regulatory_mapping.md` for global standards.

## 2. Financial Modeling & Land Strategy
- **Incentive Analysis**: Evaluate land lease options (e.g., Madayn vs. Free-Zones) and incorporate grace periods into the cash flow model.
- **CAPEX/OPEX**: Build a 5-year model including specialized machinery (Supercritical CO2, Hydrolysis reactors) and utility hubs.

## 3. Facility Design & Visualization
- **Unified Layout**: Design a single structure integrating Warehouse, Production, Labs, Utilities, and Admin.
- **Zoning Compliance**: Implement the 3-Zone cascading pressure system (Negative $\to$ Neutral $\to$ Positive).
- **Warehouse Integration**: Include Quarantine, QC Sampling, and Dispensing suites.
- **Resource**: Refer to `references/facility_zoning.md` for detailed zoning and flow standards.

## 4. Investor & Authority Presentation
- **Scientific Sustainability**: Frame the narrative around circular economy, high-science clinicals, and unique value propositions (e.g., Halal-certified collagen).
- **Visual Evidence**: Use 2D architectural plans and 3D isometric cutaways to demonstrate technical readiness.

## Workflow Summary
1. Analyze raw material and target market regulatory requirements.
2. Optimize technical process parameters for pharmaceutical-grade output.
3. Model financials including local land incentives and utility costs.
4. Design a unified, GMP-compliant facility layout.
5. Generate a high-stakes presentation script for investors and authorities.
