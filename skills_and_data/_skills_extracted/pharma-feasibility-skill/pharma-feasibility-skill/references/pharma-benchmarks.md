## Standard Financial Model Assumptions

- **Discount rate / hurdle rate**: 10–15% for emerging markets, 8–12% for developed markets
- **Project horizon for NPV**: 10 years (pharma-standard)
- **Depreciation**: Straight-line; Buildings 25yr, Equipment 10yr, IT 5yr
- **Capacity utilization ramp**: Year 1: 40–50%, Year 2: 60–70%, Year 3: 80%, Year 4+: 85–90%
- **Working capital**: Receivables 45–90 days; Inventory 60–90 days; Payables 30–60 days
- **Terminal value**: Not typically used for pharma projects unless patent-protected innovator product
