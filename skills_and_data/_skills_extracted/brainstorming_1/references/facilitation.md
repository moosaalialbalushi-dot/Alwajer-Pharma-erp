# Facilitation Techniques for Brainstorming

A neutral facilitator is essential to guide the session, enforce rules, and ensure balanced participation.

## Role of the Facilitator
- **Enforce the Rules**: Especially "no criticism" during the generation phase.
- **Equalize Participation**: Ensure everyone has a chance to contribute and prevent one or two voices from dominating.
- **Keep Momentum**: If the group gets stuck, use "prompts" or "reframing" to spark new ideas.
- **Time Management**: Keep the session focused and move to evaluation when the time is up.

## Actionable Tips
- **Set Clear Goals**: Define the specific problem to be solved at the very beginning.
- **Use Visual Aids**: Employ whiteboards, sticky notes, or digital canvases (like zemith.com) to make ideas visible and easy to organize.
- **Encourage "Yes, and..."**: Foster a positive, additive environment where ideas are built upon rather than critiqued.
- **Appoint a Note-Taker**: Ensure every idea is captured accurately without filtering.
